Introducing $LUNAR

LunarScape's native cryptocurrency

$LUNAR is a functional multi-utility BEP-20/ERC-20 token which will be used as the medium of exchange between participants on LunarScape. The goal of introducing $LUNAR is to provide a convenient and secure mode of payment and settlement between participants who interact within the ecosystem on LunarScape. Crucially, LunarScape players will be able to burn in-game gold and receive a corresponding portion of $LUNAR to a nominated wallet, as well as burn $LUNAR to receive a corresponding amount of gold in-game.

$LUNAR will be redeemable for in-game gold on a 1:1 basis.

$LUNAR will provide strong economic incentives to encourage users to exert efforts towards contribution to the growth of the LunarScape ecosystem and further a mutually beneficial system where every participant is fairly compensated for their efforts.

$LUNAR will also allow holders to create and vote on on-chain governance proposals to determine future features and/or parameters of LunarScape, with voting weight calculated in proportion to the tokens owned.
