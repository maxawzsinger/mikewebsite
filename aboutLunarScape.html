Welcome to the Moon Kingdom

LunarScape is a revolutionary new MMORPG mobile game integrated with the blockchain, allowing players to earn money while playing a simple game.

The team at LunarScape believe that the jump from basic pixelated NFT games to full-size AAA games is a jump too drastic, requiring years of development to create a playable experience. We believe there is a market for more nostalgic, tried-and-tested gameplay styles, and that LunarScape is the product that is needed to fill that gap.

Return to a golden age

LunarScape is heavily influenced by old-school RuneScape. In the Moon Kingdom, simple tasks like mining, fishing, and chopping wood will earn the player rewards, which the player can choose to spend in game or burn in exchange for our native cryptocurrency. The simplicity of these tasks allow people that have busy schedules to earn passively by playing the game.

Be challenged

More complex and difficult tasks like killing monsters, fighting bosses, and embarking on quests will provide an exciting playable aspect along with massively increased rewards for devoting more of your time to the game. Our mission is to create an experience where players can choose to either dedicate time to earn higher rewards or simply sit back and grind skills to earn more in the late-game.
